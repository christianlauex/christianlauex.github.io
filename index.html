About

Hi, I'm Christian!

I'm a Computer Engineering student, exploring the world of design and electronics.

My journey began when I was 12, with my hobbies of blaster modding, 3D printing and CAD modelling. One day, I received a custom design request. I may not have known it then, but seeing my work fulfill its purpose in someone else's life sparked something in me.

And so, I quietly dreamt of living a simple life, designing things I loved for people that loved them too.

But life is busy and it was. So I loosely held onto this dream through school, while I learnt new skills and improved old designs.

Now that I'm graduating in 2026, I'm taking my next step to bringing that dream to life!

Humanworks is my personal project, to design works for people and with people.
